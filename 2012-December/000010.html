> OK Wifi radar sees WC_Family the password is Visiting and I'm told they
> use WPA2. They also say I don't need an encryption key. It wont connect.
> Granted I'm at the far end of the building Wifi radar shows 1 bar but
> Knetwork manager (I think that's it) I'm running Kubuntu 10.4 LTS Thanks


If you have Knetworkmanager running, then you have NetworkManager running.
WiFi Radar and NetworkManager do not dance well together.  They both try to
access the wireless card and step on each other's toes.

So, assuming you uninstall NetworkManger... To use WR with WPA2, see the
directions at http://wifi-radar.berlios.de/v2.x/howto/wr-howto-wpa.html
With WPA2 I believe you can use the password as your PSK, directly.  So, in
your /etc/wpa_supplicant.conf you will want the segment:

network={
 ssid=WC_Family
 psk=Visiting
}

on the public mailing list.
